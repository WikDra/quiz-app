Quiz App Cookie Diagnostics

This page helps diagnose cookie issues in the Quiz App. It provides tools to check, set, and troubleshoot cookies used for authentication.

The diagnostics panel will automatically appear in the top-left corner of this page.

Common Cookie Issues

  • HttpOnly Cookies: These cookies can't be accessed by JavaScript, but are sent with requests. They are marked as "Present on server" but won't show in document.cookie.
  • SameSite Restrictions: Cookies with SameSite=None must also have the Secure attribute, requiring HTTPS.
  • Cross-Origin Requests: Cookies may not be sent in cross-origin requests unless credentials='include' is specified.
  • Cookie Attributes: Missing or incorrect Path, Domain, or Expires attributes can cause cookies to be rejected.

Manual Testing

You can test cookie functionality by running these commands in your browser console:

// Set a test cookie document.cookie = "test_manual=true; path=/; SameSite=None; Secure"; // Check all cookies console.log(document.cookie);
